Who We Are

A Focused Supplier for International Markets

TriDAV Impex supplies home textiles and hygiene paper products to international buyers. Our product range covers bedsheets, comforters, towels, doormats, toilet paper, kitchen rolls, tissue papers, and pocket tissues.

We work with manufacturers and suppliers to source products that meet defined quality specifications. Our focus is on consistent quality, reliable packaging, and straightforward order processes that make procurement simple for domestic and overseas buyers.

All products are packaged for safe transit and come with standard order documentation. Custom branding and private label options are available on selected product categories.
